🐑 Baba Black Sheep!

🐑 How to Play

Your Mission

A flock of sheep will flash on screen — some white, some black. Press SPACE every time you see a black sheep!

✅
Hit a black sheep

Press SPACE when a black sheep is showing → +100 points

❌
Wrong press

The game lasts exactly one minute. Exactly 15 black sheep will appear. The sheep flash faster as time goes on — stay sharp!
